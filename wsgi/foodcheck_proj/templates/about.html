About

Foodcheck.us provides users with eateries’ inspection ratings within the city of San Francisco via search or geo-location of their web browser. San Francisco is providing the merchant data, the inspections, and violations. The application architecture and infrastructure is built in a way for scalability and performance for the input of additional datasets from other cities. We hope to continue adding municipal records to our database to share with connoisseurs across the country. Bon Appétit!!!
